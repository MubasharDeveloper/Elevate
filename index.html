What do I need to do after a road traffic accident?

We understand that being in an accident can be traumatising and making a claim for compensation might seem daunting. However, winning compensation for someone else’s negligence in causing a road traffic accident can play an important role in getting your life back on track...

We might ask for some information to help process your claim. Don’t worry if you don’t have everything at first – there might be other ways that we can get the details:

  • The name and address of who was responsible for your accident
  • The registration of the responsible party’s vehicle and if possible, the name of their insurer
  • Names and contact details of any witnesses to the accident
  • If possible, photographs of where the accident happened as well as photographs of the damage to your vehicle
  • Photographs of your injuries and any other property
  • A description of the position and movements of the vehicles involved in the accident

We can help you secure the resources you need, whether that means medical treatment, financial compensation, or both. We provide a comprehensive road traffic accident claims service, including operating on a no-win, no-fee basis. Our experienced team is here to guide you every step of the way.
